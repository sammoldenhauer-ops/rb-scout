## Recommended Next Fixes

1. Expand validation denominators to include all non-projection players (count missing NFL outcomes as zero finish seasons by definition).
2. Add draft round/pick and top-12/top-24 badges directly in the player-card header.
3. Integrate Athl Agg as top-priority athletic source in pipeline and regenerate player athletic fields/ranks.
4. Reduce dependency on embedded [code](code) by rebuilding season stats/meta directly from CSV sources.
5. Add automated data validation script to assert:
   - season populations by year,
   - known spot-check values,
   - presence of required season object fields.
